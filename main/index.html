Welcome to Oslo Metropolitan University where innovation meets education!

Oslo Metropolitan University Pilestredet Campus. Photo: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:H%C3%B8gskolen_i_Oslo_og_Akershus_med_Fyrhuset.jpg

Our university is a dedicated hub for knowledge and excellence in a wide array of fields, including Computer Science, Mathematics, Engineering, Ecology and Climate Change, Biology, Energy and Transportation. We're delighted to welcome you to a world of limitless possibilities and academic exploration!

Events

Academic Conference

A multi-day academic conference that brings together researchers, scholars, and experts from around the world to present their latest findings and research papers. The event covers various fields of science and promotes knowledge exchange and collaboration.

Cultural Festival

A vibrant celebration of diversity and culture, featuring a wide range of international music, dance, food, and art. Students from various cultural backgrounds come together to showcase their heritage and share their traditions with the university community.

Sports Tournament

OsloMet often organizes sports tournaments that bring together students, faculty, and sometimes even alumni. These events can include various sports such as soccer, basketball, or even unique events like a campus-wide marathon. Sports tournaments promote physical activity, teamwork, and a sense of community among the participants.

Career Fair

An event that connects students with potential employers and internship opportunities. Companies, organizations, and recruiters set up booths and provide information about job openings, career paths, and internships.

Research Activities

Annual Technology and Innovation Expo

An event highlighting innovative research and technology projects from within the institution. Researchers, engineers, and students showcase their inventions, prototypes, and technological breakthroughs, providing a platform for collaboration and knowledge exchange.

Climate Change Impact on Ecosystems

University researchers investigate the effects of climate change on local and global ecosystems. This research may involve studying changes in temperature, weather patterns, and biodiversity, as well as assessing the impact on plant and animal species.

Research Symposium

OsloMet often hosts different types of research symposium, where students showcase their scientific research projects and findings. This can involve presentations, poster sessions, and discussions about various scientific topics. These events provide a platform for students to share their academic work, foster intellectual discussions, and encourage an interest in research and innovation.

Artificial Intelligence and Innovation

AI is used in creative fields, including art, music, and content generation, to enhance creativity and produce novel works.
