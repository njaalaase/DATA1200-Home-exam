Our university is a dedicated hub for knowledge and excellence in a wide array of fields within Computer Science. We're delighted to welcome you to a world of limitless possibilities and academic exploration!

Activities

Front-End Development

Overview of front-end development and its role in web development. Introduction to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Network Administration

Setting up and maintaining computer networks within an organization. Configuring routers, switches, and firewalls to ensure network security.Monitoring network performance and troubleshooting issues to ensure smooth operations.

Cybersecurity Management

Implementing security measures to protect computer systems, networks, and data. Conducting risk assessments and vulnerability testing. Responding to security incidents and staying informed about the latest cybersecurity threats.

Database Management

Designing, implementing, and maintaining databases to store and retrieve data efficiently.Ensuring data integrity, security, and availability.

IT Project Management

Planning, executing, and closing IT projects within a defined scope, budget, and timeline.Coordinating the efforts of cross-functional teams to achieve project goals. Managing resources, risks, and communication throughout the project lifecycle.

Modern CSS Techniques

Implementing Flexbox and Grid layout systems and much much more...
